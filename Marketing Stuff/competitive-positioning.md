# JMS Dev Lab: Competitive Positioning

**Date:** March 2026
**Based on:** All competitive research, pricing analysis, Irish market research, Shopify Experts analysis, no-code comparison

---

## Executive Summary

JMS Dev Lab occupies a genuinely underserved niche: a **solo, Cork-based developer offering fixed-price custom software for SMBs, with deep Shopify and jewelry vertical expertise**. No direct competitor combines all of these elements:

- **No Cork competitor** targets the "outgrown spreadsheets" segment
- **No Irish competitor** combines Shopify app development with custom SMB tools
- **No competitor** has 6 published Shopify apps plus custom dev services
- **No competitor** offers free calculator tools as lead generation in this market
- **GBA Solutions (Kildare)** is the closest competitor, but targets different verticals and has no Shopify expertise

The primary competitive threat is not other developers — it's **no-code platforms** (Airtable, Bubble, Glide) and **doing nothing** (staying on spreadsheets).

## 4. Messaging Gaps and Opportunities

### What Competitors Say That JMS Dev Lab Should Counter

| Competitor Message | JMS Dev Lab Counter |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| **No-code:** "Build without a developer" | "When no-code stops working, we're the next step" |
| **No-code:** "Deploy in days" | "We deliver in weeks, and it lasts for years" |
| **Agencies:** "Full team behind your project" | "No middlemen — you talk directly to the person building it" |
| **Offshore devs:** "$20/hour" | "Your builder is in Cork, speaks your language, understands your business" |
| **AI tools:** "Build it yourself with AI" | "AI writes code. We understand your business problem." |

### Messages No Competitor Is Using (Opportunities)

1. **"Your LEO can fund up to 50%"** — Nobody in the market is mentioning the Grow Digital Voucher prominently. This reframes EUR 6,000 as EUR 3,000.

2. **"Cheaper than Airtable over 3 years"** — The no-code TCO comparison is powerful and nobody is making it clearly. At 10+ users, custom is genuinely cheaper.

3. **"The jewelry industry's developer"** — Nobody claims this niche. With JewelryStudioManager, Jewel Value, and RepairDesk, JMS Dev Lab has more jewelry software than anyone in Ireland (or the UK).

4. **"From spreadsheet to system — in Cork"** — GBA Solutions uses this phrase nationally. Nobody owns it in Cork/Munster specifically.

5. **"Fixed price, or it's free"** — A bold guarantee that no competitor offers. Even a softened version ("price match guarantee against scope") would stand out.

6. **"EU-hosted, GDPR-compliant by default"** — The CLOUD Act / GDPR tension is real for EU businesses using US platforms. Nobody in the Irish SMB dev market is messaging this.

## 6. Competitive Strategy by Segment

### Against No-Code Platforms
**Strategy: "Graduate, don't compete"**
- Don't position against no-code — position as what comes after
- Create content: "Outgrown Airtable? Here's What to Do Next"
- Show 3-year TCO comparisons (custom wins at 10+ users)
- Emphasise data ownership, GDPR compliance, no lock-in
- The free tools already serve as a "taste" of custom capability

### Against Other Irish Developers
**Strategy: "Own Cork + own jewelry"**
- GBA Solutions owns "spreadsheet to system" nationally → own it in Cork/Munster
- illi has great case studies but zero content → out-content them
- RedStudio has aggressive SEO → match their location-page strategy for custom tools specifically
- Nobody targets jewelry → go deep on this vertical

### Against Shopify Expert Marketplaces
**Strategy: "Join and differentiate"**
- Register on Shopify Partner Directory (free, Ireland-based = low competition)
- Apply to Storetasker (curated, $2K-$35K/month potential)
- Differentiate with: published apps (6), niche expertise, Ireland location
- Use marketplace reviews to build social proof for the main site

### Against Enterprise Agencies
**Strategy: "Not competing — capturing their rejects"**
- SMBs who approach NearForm/Arekibo and get quoted EUR 50K+ are JMS Dev Lab's ideal prospects
- Position as "agency quality at solo developer prices"
- Target keywords like "affordable custom software Ireland" that agencies don't pursue

### Against Offshore Developers
**Strategy: "Don't compete on price"**
- Never try to match $20-30/hr offshore rates
- Emphasise: same timezone, same language, can meet in person, understands Irish business
- GDPR compliance as a genuine differentiator (not just a marketing claim)
- Fixed-price removes the rate comparison entirely — client pays for outcome, not hours

## 9. Recommended Positioning Statement

> **Custom software for small businesses. Fixed price. Built in Cork.**
> Replace your spreadsheets, connect your systems, and run your business on software that actually fits — without agency prices or platform lock-in.

### Shopify-Specific
> **Ireland's Shopify app developer.** Creator of 6 published Shopify apps for jewelry stores, retail, and SMBs. Custom Shopify apps and integrations from the developer who builds them.

### Jewelry Vertical
> **Software built for jewellers.** From commission tracking to repair management to insurance-ready valuations — purpose-built tools for jewelry businesses, by the developer who knows the industry.

### Anti-No-Code
> **Outgrown your spreadsheet? Outgrown Airtable?** Custom software costs less than you think — and less than no-code over 3 years. Fixed price, you own it, no per-seat charges.

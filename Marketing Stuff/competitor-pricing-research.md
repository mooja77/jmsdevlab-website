| Hybrid | Fixed-price discovery -> T&M build -> retainer support | Most common for larger engagements |

### 3.3 When No-Code Becomes MORE Expensive Than Custom

This is critical competitive intelligence for JMS Dev Lab:

| Factor | No-Code Cost Risk | Custom Code Advantage |
|--------|------------------|----------------------|
| Scale (50K+ users) | Platform fees $500+/month | Hosting $50-200/month on Vercel/Railway |
| Growing usage | Fees increase with storage, traffic, users | Predictable hosting costs |
| Rewrite risk | **25-30% of no-code projects rewritten within 2 years** | No platform lock-in |
| Rewrite cost | $50,000-$250,000 to rebuild | N/A |
| Migration | Rebuild from ground up required | Portable codebase |
| Performance limits | Vendor costs rise, workarounds needed | Optimise directly |
| Long-term TCO (3yr) | Platform fees compound significantly | Lower operational cost |

**The crossover point:** No-code is typically cheaper for MVPs and simple apps (<$15K budget), but custom development wins on total cost of ownership for any project expected to grow beyond basic usage or last more than 2-3 years.

## 4. Summary: Competitive Positioning for JMS Dev Lab

### Market Rate Benchmarks (GBP)

| Service | Low End (Offshore/Junior) | Mid Market | Premium (Agency/Senior) |
|---------|--------------------------|-----------|------------------------|
| Hourly rate | £15-30/hr | £50-80/hr | £100-200/hr |
| Day rate | £150-250/day | £300-500/day | £500-900/day |
| Spreadsheet replacement | £2,000-5,000 | £5,000-15,000 | £15,000-30,000 |
| Client portal (basic) | £8,000-15,000 | £15,000-40,000 | £40,000-80,000 |
| Shopify custom app (simple) | £3,000-8,000 | £8,000-20,000 | £20,000-50,000 |
| Internal tool / dashboard | £5,000-15,000 | £15,000-40,000 | £40,000-100,000 |

### Key Takeaways

1. **The solo dev sweet spot is £50-80/hr (£300-500/day).** This is where experienced UK/Ireland freelancers sit. Below this looks cheap/risky; above this competes with agencies.

2. **Fixed-price packages are attractive to SMBs.** Clients prefer predictable costs. Most agencies still default to T&M, creating an opportunity for fixed-price positioning.

3. **Spreadsheet replacement is under-priced as a category.** Most UK firms offering this service charge only for Excel consultancy (£110/hr). Actual custom software to replace spreadsheets (web apps, databases) commands £5,000-30,000 but few solo devs package it clearly.

4. **No-code agencies are NOT necessarily cheaper.** At $100-200/hr for Bubble agencies plus ongoing platform fees, custom development can be more cost-effective for anything beyond a basic MVP. The 25-30% rewrite rate within 2 years is a powerful selling point for custom code.

5. **Shopify app development is a premium niche.** Even basic custom apps start at $5,000. JMS Dev Lab's existing Shopify app portfolio (6 published apps) provides credibility that most freelancers lack.

6. **Maintenance/retainer revenue is significant.** Agencies charge £378-504/day on retainer. Even modest monthly support packages (e.g., 2-4 days/month) represent £750-2,000/month recurring revenue.

7. **Upwork/offshore creates a price floor but not real competition for local SMBs.** The median Upwork rate of $20/hr is dominated by offshore devs. UK/Ireland SMBs hiring custom developers locally expect to pay £50-100/hr and value communication, timezone, and accountability.

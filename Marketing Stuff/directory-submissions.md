# Directory Submissions Guide — JMS Dev Lab Apps

## Overview

Submitting apps to software directories delivers three things at zero cost:

1. **Backlinks** — Every directory profile links back to your app website, improving domain authority and SEO.
2. **Discovery** — People actively searching for software in these directories have buying intent. They're comparing options and ready to install.
3. **Social proof** — Being listed alongside established tools signals legitimacy. A G2 or Capterra profile adds credibility when prospects research you.

Most directories offer free listings. The time investment is 15-30 minutes per app per directory. Do it once, benefit for years.

- **URL:** https://www.saashub.com/
- **Cost:** Free
- **How it works:** Submit your product for a free profile. SaaSHub curates alternatives and comparisons.
- **Notes:** Fill out pricing, features, and category carefully. Takes 1-2 weeks for approval. Submit all 7 apps.

## Product Hunt Launch Strategy — Pitch Side

Launch Pitch Side first. It's free, it has a good story, and free products perform well on Product Hunt.

### Why Pitch Side first:
- Free products get more upvotes (no barrier to try)
- The story is relatable — "I'm a volunteer coach, I built what I needed"
- It's a genuine passion project, which Product Hunt's community respects
- If the launch goes well, it creates awareness for JMS Dev Lab's other products

### Launch details:

**Product name:** Pitch Side

**Tagline (60 chars max):** Free coaching companion for grassroots football

**Topics:** Sports, Coaching, Football, Free, Productivity

**Short description:**
Pitch Side is a free app for the thousands of volunteer coaches who run grassroots football teams on evenings and weekends. Plan sessions, track players, build formations, and organise your season. No premium tier, no ads, no catch.

**Maker comment draft:**

> Hi Product Hunt! I'm the maker of Pitch Side.
>
> I coach a grassroots football team — the kind where you're juggling training plans on your phone between the school run and a 7pm session in the rain. Every coaching app I found was either built for professional clubs (and priced accordingly) or abandoned after a few updates.
>
> So I built what I actually needed: a simple, fast tool for planning sessions, tracking player development, and keeping my season organised. Something I could use on the sideline, on my phone, without a tutorial.
>
> Pitch Side is completely free. No premium tier, no feature gates, no ads. I build software for a living (Shopify apps, custom tools for businesses), and this is my way of giving back to the grassroots coaching community.
>
> I'd love feedback from anyone who coaches, or who knows someone who does. What features would make your coaching life easier?

### Launch timing:
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday
- **Time:** Post at 00:01 PST (08:01 GMT / 09:01 Irish time) to maximise the full 24-hour window
- **Avoid:** Mondays (competition from weekend launches), Fridays (lower traffic), weekends

### Pre-launch preparation:
- [ ] Create a Product Hunt maker account
- [ ] Set up the Pitch Side product page as a draft
- [ ] Prepare 4-5 high-quality screenshots/GIFs showing key features
- [ ] Write the maker comment (draft above, refine before launch)
- [ ] Have the Pitch Side website updated with a "Featured on Product Hunt" section ready to activate
- [ ] Prepare social media posts to share on launch day
- [ ] Notify anyone you know who has a Product Hunt account (but do not ask for upvotes — that violates PH rules)

### After launch:
- Respond to every comment personally
- Share the launch on social media channels
- If it gets traction, consider launching SmartCash or GrowthMap next (4-6 weeks later)
- Add "Featured on Product Hunt" badge to the Pitch Side website

## Submission Order

Prioritise submissions in this order:

1. **AlternativeTo** — Quick to submit, immediate backlinks, all 7 apps
2. **Capterra** — High-authority backlink, free, all 7 apps (feeds into GetApp and Software Advice)
3. **G2** — Strong SEO authority, start with JSM, SmartCash, StaffHub
4. **SaaSHub** — Easy submission, all 7 apps
5. **Product Hunt** — Launch Pitch Side (requires preparation, schedule when ready)
6. **SourceForge** — Lower priority, all 7 apps
7. **Irish directories** — GoldenPages, BusinessListings, TechIreland (JMS Dev Lab brand)

Estimated total time: 4-6 hours spread across a few sessions. The descriptions and details above are ready to copy-paste, which should speed things up considerably.

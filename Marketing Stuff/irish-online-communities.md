# Irish Online Communities for Business & Tech

**Created:** 15 March 2026
**Purpose:** Places to build visibility, answer questions, and share expertise — all written/online, no in-person required.

---

## Priority 1: Join Now (Shy-Compatible, Written Only)

### Irish Tech Community (Slack)
- **URL:** https://irishtechcommunity.com/
- **What:** Ireland's main tech Slack workspace. Developers, makers, engineers, startups.
- **Channels:** Dev, design, startups, conferences, Cork-specific chat
- **How to join:** Free — request invite on website
- **Strategy:** Join, lurk for a week, then start answering questions about Shopify dev, custom software, spreadsheet replacement. Share blog posts when genuinely relevant.
- **Why it matters:** Direct access to potential referral partners (other devs who don't do custom SMB tools) and potential clients (startup founders, business owners in tech).

### Boards.ie — Entrepreneurial & Business Management
- **URL:** https://www.boards.ie/categories/entrepreneurial-business-management
- **What:** Ireland's largest forum. Active threads about starting businesses, software, outsourcing, grants.
- **Strategy:** Answer questions like "should I build an app or use Airtable?", "how much does custom software cost?", "best software for [niche]". Link to blog posts where relevant. Don't self-promote — just be helpful.
- **Key threads to monitor:** Software recommendations, spreadsheet problems, Shopify questions, LEO grant discussions.
- **Also check:** https://www.boards.ie/categories/business-finance-business (Business & Finance section)

### AskAboutBusiness (AskAboutMoney.com)
- **URL:** https://www.askaboutmoney.com/forums/askaboutbusiness.12/
- **What:** Sub-forum of Ireland's biggest personal finance site. Business owners discussing tax, funding, operations, company law.
- **Strategy:** More finance/operations focused. Good for answering questions about software costs, digital vouchers, LEO grants.
- **Audience:** SMB owners, sole traders, people starting businesses — exactly your target market.

## Content Strategy Across All Communities

### Questions to Watch For (and Answer)
- "Can anyone recommend a developer?"
- "We've outgrown our spreadsheet, what do we do?"
- "How much does it cost to build an app?"
- "Shopify app recommendations for [niche]?"
- "No-code vs custom — which should I choose?"
- "Has anyone used the LEO digital voucher?"
- "Best software for jewelry/repair/training/cashflow?"

### How to Answer
1. Give a genuinely helpful, complete answer
2. Don't mention JMS Dev Lab unless directly asked
3. If you have a relevant blog post, link it naturally: "I wrote about this in detail here: [link]"
4. Be honest — if SaaS is the right answer, say so
5. Build reputation over weeks, not days

### What NOT to Do
- Don't join and immediately post "Hi, I'm a developer, hire me"
- Don't paste the same promotional message in multiple groups
- Don't argue with people who disagree
- Don't share links in every reply — only when genuinely useful
- Don't create threads about your own services

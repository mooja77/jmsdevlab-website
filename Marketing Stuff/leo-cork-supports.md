## Detailed Breakdown

### 1. Feasibility/Innovation Grant
- **What:** Grant to investigate market viability of a proposed product/service. Covers prototype, research, patent costs, consultancy, own labour (max EUR 400/week, capped at 20%).
- **Amount:** Up to 50% of costs, max **EUR 15,000** (excl. VAT).
- **Eligibility:** Micro-enterprises (max 10 employees) in manufacturing or internationally traded services, Cork City. Project must not have started before LEO approval.
- **Exclusion risk:** "Professional services (including consultancies)" are excluded. Custom dev services could be interpreted this way. Frame applications around Shopify app products, not services.
- **URL:** localenterprise.ie/corkcity/Financial-Supports/Feasibility-Grant/

### 2. Priming Grant
- **What:** Start-up grant for micro-enterprises in first 18 months. Covers capital, salary, utilities, marketing, consultancy, training.
- **Amount:** Up to 50% of investment, max **EUR 50,000**. Job creation grants up to EUR 15,000/position (roles >EUR 40,000/year).
- **Eligibility:** Same as Feasibility + must be within first 18 months of trading.
- **URL:** localenterprise.ie/corkcity/Financial-Supports/Priming-Grant/

### 3. LEO Mentor Programme
- **What:** Free 1-to-1 mentoring. Covers business strategy, financial planning, marketing, market research, website planning.
- **Eligibility:** New and existing businesses in Cork City. No sector exclusions.
- **Cost:** Free.
- **URL:** localenterprise.ie/Discover-Business-Supports/Mentoring/

### 4. Digital for Business
- **What:** Access to expert digital consultant who analyses your digital systems and identifies gaps.
- **Eligibility:** 1-50 employees, trading 6+ months, not EI/IDA client. Sole traders eligible.
- **Prerequisite for:** Grow Digital Voucher.

### 5. Grow Digital Voucher
- **What:** Up to **EUR 5,000** (50% of eligible costs) for adopting digital tech. Software subscriptions (new to business, max 1 year), e-commerce, CRM, cybersecurity.
- **Prerequisite:** Must have completed Digital for Business first.
- **Exclusion:** Bespoke software and regulatory compliance systems don't qualify.

### 6. Innovation Voucher (via Enterprise Ireland)
- **What:** **EUR 10,000** standard (or up to EUR 20,000 co-funded) to work with registered knowledge provider (university, research body).
- **Eligibility:** SMEs, must be registered limited company. **Sole traders not eligible.**
- **Opportunity:** Collaborate with MTU Cork on AI/ML for SmartCash forecasting or valuation algorithms for Jewel Value.
- **URL:** enterprise-ireland.com/en/supports/innovation-voucher/

### 7. Market Explorer Grant
- **What:** Up to **EUR 10,000** (or 50% of costs) for exploring new geographic markets.
- **Eligibility:** 1-50 employees, trading 12+ months, manufacturing or internationally traded services.

### 8. Microfinance Ireland
- **What:** Unsecured business loans EUR 2,000–25,000 at 5.5% APR (via LEO, vs 6.5% standard).
- **Eligibility:** Under 10 employees, turnover up to EUR 2M. **Sole traders eligible.**
- **Includes:** Free mentoring upon approval.

## Key Strategic Point

The biggest eligibility question: do JMS Dev Lab's Shopify apps qualify as "internationally traded services"? Apps sold globally via the Shopify App Store = strong argument. Custom dev services may be classified as excluded "professional services/consultancy."

**Frame all discussions with LEO around the Shopify app products, not custom dev services.**

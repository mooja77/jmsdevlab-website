# Online Networking & Communities for JMS Dev Lab

All options below can be engaged with entirely online -- no podcasts, meetups, or speaking required.

## Priority Order
1. LEO Cork City (free mentoring + grants)
2. Indie Hackers Shopify App Developers (free, directly relevant)
3. Shopify Partners Slack + Developer Forums (free, essential)
4. Irish Tech Community Slack (free, low-pressure)
5. MicroConf Connect (paid, high-quality -- when revenue justifies it)

---

## 1. LEO Cork City (Local Enterprise Office) -- HIGH PRIORITY
- **URL:** localenterprise.ie/corkcity/
- **What:** Free mentoring, grants, online training
- **Key offerings:**
  - **Feasibility Grant** -- funding to research market demand
  - **Priming Grant** -- startup grant for micro-enterprises (first 18 months)
  - **Free mentoring** -- 1-to-1 via phone/WhatsApp/Skype/Teams/Zoom
  - **Online training** -- "Boost your Business Online" digital marketing, 1.5hrs/week x 6 weeks
- **In-person required?** No
- **Cost:** Free
- **Contact:** info@leo.corkcity.ie / 021 4961828

## 2. Indie Hackers -- Shopify App Developers Group -- VERY HIGH
- **URL:** indiehackers.com/group/shopify-app-developers
- **What:** Community of indie devs building Shopify apps. Revenue sharing, strategies, marketing tactics.
- **In-person required?** No
- **Cost:** Free

## 3. Shopify Partners Slack -- VERY HIGH
- **URL:** shopify.com/partners/community
- **What:** Official Slack workspace for Shopify Partners. Connect with other devs and Shopify team.
- **In-person required?** No
- **Cost:** Free (already a Shopify Partner)

## 4. Shopify Developer Community Forums -- HIGH
- **URL:** community.shopify.dev
- **What:** Official dev forums. Technical discussions, bug reports, feature requests. Build reputation by helping others.
- **In-person required?** No
- **Cost:** Free

## 5. Irish Tech Community (Slack) -- HIGH
- **URL:** irishtechcommunity.com
- **How to join:** Email join@irishtechcommunity.com for invite
- **What:** Free Slack community. Channels for design, dev, frameworks, jobs, casual topics.
- **In-person required?** No
- **Cost:** Free

## 6. MicroConf Connect -- HIGH (when budget allows)
- **URL:** microconf.com/connect
- **What:** Curated online community of bootstrapped SaaS founders. Run by Rob Walling.
- **In-person required?** No (conferences are optional)
- **Cost:** $49/month or $499/year

## 7. Scale Ireland -- MODERATE
- **URL:** scaleireland.com
- **What:** 800+ members, Slack community of 1,000+ founders/investors. 500K+ in partner discounts.
- **In-person required?** Slack is online; events are optional
- **Cost:** Contact martina@scaleireland.org

## 8. Republic of Work Cork -- LOW
- **URL:** republicofwork.com/membership
- **What:** Virtual Office membership (business address, no attendance needed). But networking is in-person.
- **In-person required?** Virtual office: no. Networking: yes.
- **Cost:** Enquire directly

## 9. Enterprise Ireland -- LOW (for now)
- **URL:** enterprise-ireland.com/en/supports/start-ups
- **What:** Feasibility funding up to 30K EUR, HPSU investment up to 800K EUR.
- **In-person required?** Applications can be remote.
- **Best for:** If an app shows breakout international growth.

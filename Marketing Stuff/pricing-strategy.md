# JMS Dev Lab: Pricing Strategy

**Date:** March 2026
**Based on:** Competitor pricing research, Shopify Experts analysis, Irish dev market research, no-code vs custom comparison, SMB hiring research

---

## Executive Summary

JMS Dev Lab should position at the **mid-market level** for Irish/UK solo developers, with **published indicative pricing** as a key differentiator. Research shows:

- Irish senior freelance devs charge EUR 75-130/hr (Cork ~5-10% below Dublin)
- The closest Irish competitor (GBA Solutions, Kildare) publishes prices: EUR 3,000-12,000+
- 90%+ of agencies hide pricing behind "contact us" — publishing prices is a trust advantage
- Fixed-price is increasingly expected by SMBs and differentiates from hourly agencies
- No-code with a consultant costs nearly the same as custom development, but custom wins on TCO

## 2. Recommended Positioning: Mid-Market Value

JMS Dev Lab should position as **mid-market with value emphasis** — not budget, not premium.

**Why not budget:**
- Signals low quality; competes with offshore devs at $20/hr
- Attracts price-sensitive clients who are harder to work with
- Unsustainable margins as a solo developer

**Why not premium:**
- Solo dev can't command agency-level pricing without agency-level social proof
- Target market (SMBs) is inherently budget-conscious
- Need to build credibility first — premium positioning comes with track record

**Why mid-market with value:**
- Aligns with GBA Solutions pricing (proven in Irish market)
- Below agency rates (EUR 100+/hr) but above offshore
- Fixed-price model provides value guarantee that hourly doesn't
- "You get the person who builds it, not an account manager"
- Can compete on total cost of ownership vs no-code

## 3. Recommended Package Pricing

### Package 1: Starter — "Replace Your Spreadsheet"
**Target:** Business running on spreadsheets/Excel, 1-10 users
**Price: EUR 3,000-6,000** (fixed price)
**Timeline:** 3-4 weeks
**Includes:**
- Discovery call + requirements document
- Custom web application replacing one core spreadsheet workflow
- User accounts + role-based access
- Basic reporting/dashboard
- Mobile-friendly design
- Deployment + 30 days post-launch support
- EU-hosted (GDPR compliant)

**Justification:**
- GBA Solutions charges EUR 3,000-7,000 for equivalent
- Below the agency minimum of EUR 10,000-30,000
- Above the "suspiciously cheap" threshold
- Aligns with LEO Grow Digital Voucher (50% funded up to EUR 5,000)
- 3-year TCO: EUR 3,360-6,360 (build + hosting) vs EUR 3,600-14,400 for Airtable

### Package 2: Growth — "Build Your Business Tool"
**Target:** Business needing multi-feature internal tool or client-facing portal
**Price: EUR 6,000-12,000** (fixed price)
**Timeline:** 4-8 weeks
**Includes:**
- Everything in Starter, plus:
- Multiple user roles + permissions
- Client/customer portal capability
- Integration with one external system (Shopify, email, accounting)
- Advanced reporting + data export
- Training session for team
- 60 days post-launch support

**Justification:**
- GBA Solutions' mid-tier: EUR 7,000-12,000
- Shopify custom app (simple): market rate EUR 3,000-15,000
- Client portals: budget tier starts at EUR 8,000-15,000 from agencies
- This is the sweet spot — complex enough to need custom, affordable enough for SMBs

### Package 3: Scale — "Custom Software, Built to Fit"
**Target:** Business needing end-to-end custom solution, complex integrations, or Shopify app
**Price: EUR 12,000-25,000** (fixed price, scoped per project)
**Timeline:** 8-12 weeks
**Includes:**
- Everything in Growth, plus:
- Complex business logic / domain-specific features
- Multiple system integrations
- Custom API development
- Advanced automation
- Full documentation
- 90 days post-launch support
- Priority support retainer option

**Justification:**
- GBA Solutions: EUR 12,000+ for complex solutions
- UK agencies: EUR 15,000-40,000 for equivalent
- Shopify custom app (mid-complexity): market rate EUR 8,000-20,000
- Positions below agencies but delivers comparable quality

### Ongoing Support Retainer (Optional Add-On)
**Price: EUR 300-800/month**
- 4-8 hours of development time per month
- Bug fixes, feature updates, minor enhancements
- Priority response time
- Monthly status call

**Justification:**
- Storetasker retainers: $1,000-3,000/month
- UK agency retainers: GBP 378-504/day (EUR 4,000-6,000+/month)
- JMS Dev Lab retainer is significantly below agency rates
- Recurring revenue stream that builds long-term relationships

## 6. Pricing Presentation Strategy

### On the Website
- **Lead with packages, not hourly rates.** SMBs think in project terms, not hours.
- **Show starting prices, not exact prices.** "From EUR 3,000" signals ballpark without committing to every scope.
- **Include the LEO voucher message.** "Your project could be 50% funded" is a powerful call to action.
- **Show the no-code comparison.** A simple table showing 3-year TCO builds the case for custom.
- **Add a "not sure which package?" CTA** leading to a free discovery call.

### In Sales Conversations
- Start with understanding the problem, not quoting a price.
- Use the no-code comparison to justify custom development.
- Mention the LEO Grow Digital Voucher early — it reframes the price.
- Quote fixed price based on scoped requirements, within the package ranges.
- Offer milestone payments: 30% upfront, 40% at mid-point, 30% at delivery.

### What NOT to Do
- Don't publish hourly rates — it invites comparison with offshore devs.
- Don't offer discounts — it signals the original price was too high.
- Don't price below EUR 2,500 for any project — it attracts bad clients and isn't sustainable.
- Don't hide pricing entirely — this is the #1 trust builder the research identified.

## 4. Testimonial Request Templates

### Email Template — For Shopify App Users

**Subject:** Quick favour — would you share your experience?

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed you've been using [App Name] and I hope it's been helpful for your business.

I'm building out the JMS Dev Lab website and would love to include a short quote from a real user. If you've had a positive experience, would you be willing to share a sentence or two I could use?

Something simple like:
- What problem the app solved for you
- How it's helped your workflow
- Whether you'd recommend it

No pressure at all — and I'm happy to keep it anonymous if you prefer (e.g., "Jewelry store owner, London").

If you have a moment, just reply with a line or two. It really helps.

Thanks,
[Your name]
JMS Dev Lab
```

### Shorter Version — For In-App or Follow-Up

```
Hi [Name] — I'm adding testimonials to the JMS Dev Lab website. If [App Name] has been useful for your business, would you mind sharing a quick sentence I could use? Happy to keep it anonymous if you prefer. No pressure either way!
```

### Shopify App Store Review Request

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for using [App Name]! If you've found it helpful, would you mind leaving a quick review on the Shopify App Store? Even a sentence or two makes a big difference for a small developer.

Here's the direct link: [App Store URL]

Thanks so much,
[Your name]
```

### Where to Display Testimonials

Once you have 3-5 quotes, add them to:
1. **Homepage** — between the "By the Numbers" and "What We Build" sections
2. **Pricing page** — above the FAQ section
3. **Case studies page** — alongside relevant app case studies
4. **Shopify Partner Directory** — as work samples/portfolio

## Recommended Order

1. **Shopify Partner Directory** (5 minutes) — you likely already have a Partners account from publishing apps. Just fill in the profile.
2. **Storetasker** (10 minutes) — application, they review and respond within a week or two.
3. **Google Business Profile** (15 minutes) — setup + wait for verification postcard.
4. **Testimonial requests** (10 minutes) — send to your most active app users.

All four can be done in under an hour.

# Show HN Post — Pitch Side

## Title
Show HN: Pitch Side – Free coaching app for grassroots football (soccer) volunteers

## Body

Hi HN,

I'm a solo dev in Cork, Ireland. I built Pitch Side (https://pitchsideapp.net) — a free web app for volunteer grassroots football (soccer) coaches.

Grassroots football runs on volunteers. Parents who stepped up, teachers who started after-school clubs, locals who've been running the under-12s for years. These people give their time, energy, and often their own money for kit. The coaching tools that exist are either built for professionals with professional price tags, or free but ad-riddled and half-finished.

Pitch Side is what I wished existed when I was coaching. It covers the basics that actually matter:

- Squad management (players, positions, contacts, medical notes)
- Session planning with drag-and-drop drills and timing
- 75+ pre-built drills (passing, shooting, dribbling, defending) plus custom drills
- Formation builder for 5v5, 7v7, 9v9, and 11v11
- Match day tools — lineups, substitution rotations, printable team sheets
- Season analytics — attendance trends, drill usage, training frequency

It's a Next.js web app with Firebase for cloud storage/sync. Works on phones, tablets, and laptops — no app store install needed. Your data syncs across devices.

Completely free. No accounts required, no ads, no tracking, no premium tier, no upsell. I build paid Shopify apps for a living, so this doesn't need to make money. It's a genuine goodwill project for people who deserve a decent tool without another subscription.

Would love feedback from anyone — whether you coach, play, or just have opinions on the UX. Happy to answer questions about the build too.

https://pitchsideapp.net

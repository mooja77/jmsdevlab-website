Common Questions

What People Usually Ask

How much does custom software cost?

Most projects fall between €3,000 and €25,000. A spreadsheet replacement typically starts at €3,000. Client portals and multi-feature tools start at €6,000. Complex custom solutions start at €12,000. We'll have a free conversation about what you need, then give you a clear, fixed quote before any work starts. No surprises. See our pricing page for full details.

How long does a project take?

Simple tools can be ready in a few weeks. Larger systems take longer. We'll give you an honest timeline upfront, and you'll see working software throughout — not just at the end.

I don't know exactly what I need — is that okay?

Absolutely. Most people come to us with a problem, not a specification. "We're drowning in spreadsheets" or "our clients keep ringing for updates" is plenty to start with. We'll help you figure out what the right solution looks like.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. You know your business and your problems — that's what matters. We handle all the technical decisions and explain things in plain English.

Have you built custom software for other businesses?

We've built multiple production applications that are live and used by real businesses every day — you can see them on this page. We're now offering that same capability to other businesses who need custom tools built. Same team, same standards, same tech stack.

What happens after launch? Do you just disappear?

No. We provide ongoing support and maintenance. The people who built your software are the same people who support it. If something needs fixing or you want to add features later, we're here.

Will I own the software you build?

Yes. It's your software. You own the code, the data, everything. No vendor lock-in, no per-seat licensing that creeps up every year. We build it, you own it.

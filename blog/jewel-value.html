Jewel Value: Professional Jewelry Valuations Made Simple

5 February 2026

The Problem

Jewelry valuations are a core revenue stream for many retail jewelers, but the process behind them is often painfully manual. Handwritten certificates, inconsistent formatting, no easy way for customers to access their documents after the fact, and zero visibility into how the valuation side of the business is actually performing.

Insurance companies need specific information in specific formats. Customers expect a professional document they can trust. And jewelers need to produce all of this quickly, without spending half an hour per certificate formatting a Word document.

Why We Built It

We'd already built JewelryStudioManager and understood the jewelry industry well. Valuations kept coming up in conversations — it was a pain point that nobody had solved properly, especially not within the Shopify ecosystem.

The existing options were either expensive desktop software from the 1990s or completely manual processes. We saw an opportunity to bring valuations into the modern era — fast, professional, and integrated with the tools jewelers already use.

What It Does

Jewel Value turns what used to be a slow, manual process into something a jeweler can complete in minutes:

  • Professional certificates — Customisable valuation certificates with your branding, exported as polished PDFs. They look like they came from a high-end firm, not a home printer.
  • Insurance-ready documentation — Every certificate includes the fields insurance companies require. No more back-and-forth about missing information.
  • Fast workflow — Streamlined input screens let you complete a full valuation in minutes, not hours. The app guides you through each step.
  • Customer portal — Customers get secure online access to view and download their certificates anytime. No more "can you send that again?" emails.
  • Analytics & reporting — Track how many valuations you're doing, your revenue from the service, and how your team is performing.
  • Shopify integration — Product syncing and automatic valuation creation from your existing catalogue.

The Pricing

We structured the pricing to work for jewelers of all sizes. The Basic plan starts at $9.99/mo for shops doing a handful of valuations. Professional at $29.99/mo adds API access, advanced branding, and supports up to 10 team members. Enterprise at $59.99/mo gives you unlimited everything plus white-label options and dedicated support.

Every plan comes with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. We wanted jewelers to see the value before they pay a penny.

Who It's For

Retail jewelers who offer valuation services and want to look professional, work faster, and give their customers a better experience. Whether you do five valuations a month or five hundred, Jewel Value scales with you.

Jewel Value is live on the Shopify App Store, starting at $9.99/mo.

SmartCash: Cashflow Management for Shopify Stores

14 February 2026

The Problem

Most Shopify store owners can tell you their revenue. Very few can tell you their actual cash position, their runway, or whether they can afford to place that next inventory order. The financial side of running a store is often a black box — money comes in, money goes out, and at the end of the month you hope there's something left.

The tools that exist for financial management are either full accounting platforms (overkill for most store owners) or basic spreadsheet templates that require hours of manual data entry every week.

Why We Built It

We kept hearing the same frustrations from Shopify merchants: "I don't know if I'm actually profitable," "Month-end accounting takes forever," "I don't know how long my cash will last," and "I'm making decisions based on gut feeling."

SmartCash was built to bridge the gap between "I have a Shopify store" and "I understand my finances." Not by replacing your accountant, but by giving you real-time visibility into the numbers that matter most — without the manual work.

What It Does

SmartCash connects to your Shopify store and automatically turns your order data into financial intelligence:

  • Executive dashboard — Your cash position, revenue trends, expenses, and runway at a glance. Open the app and know where you stand in seconds.
  • Automatic order sync — Every Shopify order is pulled in and categorised automatically. No manual data entry, no copy-pasting from exports.
  • AI-powered forecasting — ARIMA-based revenue predictions with confidence intervals. See where your cash flow is heading, not just where it's been.
  • What-if scenarios — Thinking about hiring someone? Launching a marketing campaign? Test the financial impact before you commit.
  • 15+ financial reports — Profit & loss, revenue breakdowns, trend analysis, and more. All exportable to CSV for your accountant.
  • Multi-store support — Run multiple Shopify stores? See them all in one place, or drill down into each individually.

AI That Actually Helps

We're cautious about slapping "AI" on everything, but in this case it genuinely adds value. The ARIMA forecasting model analyses your historical order patterns and generates revenue predictions with confidence intervals. It's not magic — it's statistical modelling done properly, and it gets more accurate the more data it has.

The what-if scenario engine uses the same models to let you test decisions. "What happens to my runway if I increase ad spend by 20%?" — you get an answer in seconds instead of guessing.

Who It's For

Shopify store owners who want to understand their finances without becoming accountants. Whether you're a solo founder trying to figure out if you can afford to quit your day job, or a growing team that needs real financial visibility — SmartCash gives you the numbers without the complexity.

SmartCash is live on the Shopify App Store, starting at $9.99/mo.

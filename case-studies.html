Shopify App • Jewelry Industry

JewelryStudioManager

The CRM that jewelry studios never had

The Problem

Custom jewelry studios tracked commissions in spreadsheets with dozens of tabs. Client preferences (ring sizes, metal choices, design history) were scattered across emails, notebooks, and memory. When a client called about their piece, staff scrambled to find the details.

What We Built

A purpose-built CRM that understands the jewelry commission workflow: inquiry → consultation → design approval → production → delivery. Client profiles store every preference. A branded portal lets customers check progress without calling.

Shopify App • Jewelry & Watch Repair

RepairDesk

Repair management for jewellers and watchmakers

The Problem

Repair shops tracked jobs on paper tickets or basic spreadsheets. Customers had no way to check repair status. Workshop staff couldn't see at a glance what was in progress, what was waiting for parts, or what was overdue.

What We Built

A repair management system with custom workflow stages, photo documentation per job, automatic status notifications to customers, and a dashboard showing the entire workshop at a glance. Integrated directly with Shopify for customer data.

Shopify App • Financial Management

SmartCash

Cashflow management with AI forecasting

The Problem

Shopify store owners knew their revenue but not their cashflow. Bills, subscriptions, and seasonal dips caught them off guard. They couldn't answer the simple question: "Can I afford to place this order?"

What We Built

A cashflow management app that syncs with Shopify revenue data, tracks recurring expenses, and uses AI to forecast future cashflow. Visual dashboards show exactly when cash will be tight, so owners can plan ahead.

Shopify App • Jewelry Valuations

Jewel Value

Professional valuations made simple

The Problem

Jewellers created valuation certificates manually in Word or PDF templates. Each certificate took 20–30 minutes. Customers had no easy way to access or share their certificates with insurers. There was no central record.

What We Built

A streamlined valuation system that generates professional, customisable certificates in minutes. Insurance-ready PDFs, a customer portal for viewing and downloading, and complete analytics on valuation history and revenue.

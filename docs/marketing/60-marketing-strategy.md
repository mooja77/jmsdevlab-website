## 1. Revenue Streams

JMS Dev Lab operates two distinct revenue streams, each requiring different marketing approaches.

| Stream | Description | Pricing | Status |
|--------|-------------|---------|--------|
| SaaS Apps | 9 production apps (7 Shopify embedded, 2 standalone) sold as monthly subscriptions | $4.99--$149/mo per app | Pre-revenue; 6 apps submitted for Shopify review |
| Custom Development | Bespoke software built for businesses replacing spreadsheets, internal tools, or needing custom portals | EUR 3,000--12,000 fixed price | Pre-revenue; Vegrify RFQ (EUR 28,875) pending |

### SaaS marketing priorities

- Get Shopify apps approved and listed in the App Store (organic discovery)
- SEO-driven content targeting long-tail keywords (e.g., "best Shopify cash flow app", "jewelry CRM software")
- Comparison blog posts against established competitors (Triffin, BriteCo, EasyTeam, RepairPilot)

### Custom dev marketing priorities

- Directory presence on freelance and agency listing platforms
- Government procurement (eTenders, public-procurement.ie)
- SEO content targeting decision-stage keywords (e.g., "replace spreadsheet with app", "custom software cost Ireland")
- Community engagement in business forums

## 3. Positioning

### Core positioning statement

JMS Dev Lab is a solo software development practice run by a business owner who builds software for other business owners. EU-based, GDPR-compliant, fixed-price, with 9 shipped production apps as proof of capability.

### Differentiators

| Differentiator | Why it matters |
|----------------|----------------|
| 9 shipped apps | Proof of delivery -- not promises, finished products |
| Business owner background | 22 years running Moores Jewellers means genuine understanding of retail pain points |
| Solo developer, direct access | Clients talk to the person who writes the code -- no project managers, no telephone game |
| Fixed pricing | EUR 3K / 6K / 12K tiers -- clients know the cost before they commit |
| EU-based, GDPR-compliant | Important for Irish/EU clients, government procurement, and data-sensitive industries |
| Jewelry industry expertise | Niche credibility -- three apps specifically for jewellers (Jewel Value, RepairDesk, JewelryStudioManager) |
| Scope-match guarantee | If the delivered product does not match the agreed scope, the client does not pay the final instalment |
| No VAT | Not VAT registered (below EUR 42,500 threshold) -- saves clients 23% compared to VAT-registered competitors |

### Target audiences

| Audience | Channel | Offer |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Shopify merchants | Shopify App Store, blog SEO, r/shopify | SaaS apps ($4.99--$149/mo) |
| Small businesses replacing spreadsheets | Blog SEO, directories, Bark | Custom dev (EUR 3K--6K) |
| Irish SMEs needing internal tools | Directories, LEO voucher, eTenders | Custom dev (EUR 6K--12K) |
| Government / public sector | eTenders, public-procurement.ie | Custom dev (project-based) |
| Jewelry businesses | Blog SEO, JHJ group (no advertising), niche keywords | SaaS apps + custom dev |
| Charities and sports clubs | Direct outreach (post-May 2026) | Discounted/pro-bono for portfolio |

## 4. Constraints

These constraints are non-negotiable and shape every marketing decision.

| Constraint | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| No paid marketing spend | No paid upgrades, ads, or premium listings until JMS Dev Lab has revenue |
| Shy founder | John avoids public-facing marketing: no podcasts, meetups, conferences, speaking engagements, or on-camera video content |
| No LinkedIn until ~May 2026 | LinkedIn profile update deferred until Moores Jewellers closes to avoid confusion |
| Real content only | No fake testimonials, placeholder data, or inflated claims -- everything must be factual and verifiable |
| No free app tiers | 14-day free trial only -- business cannot afford free users at this stage |
| Proactive outreach paused | LEO Cork applications, agency cold emails, community posting paused until Moores Jewellers closes (~May 2026) |
| Inbound is fair game | Responding to RFQs, Bark leads, and direct enquiries is not paused |
| Pre-revenue | Operating on savings; every marketing activity must be zero-cost |

## 5. Channel Priority

Channels are ranked by expected return on time invested, given the constraints above.

| Priority | Channel | Rationale |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | SEO / content marketing | Compounds over time, works while sleeping, no cost, suits written-content preference |
| 2 | Shopify App Store | Once approved, organic discovery with high purchase intent |
| 3 | Directories (Upwork, Bark, Referr, Clutch, Sortlist) | Capture active buyers, pay-per-lead or free listings |
| 4 | Government procurement (eTenders) | High-value contracts, structured process, plays to strengths |
| 5 | Community engagement (Reddit, boards.ie, dev.to, Quora) | Build karma and credibility, drive indirect traffic |
| 6 | Email marketing (MailerLite drip) | Nurture leads who land on site, automated |
| 7 | Social media (Facebook, Instagram) | Maintain presence, not primary driver |
| 8 | LinkedIn | Deferred until ~May 2026 |
| 9 | Paid advertising | Last resort -- only when revenue justifies spend |

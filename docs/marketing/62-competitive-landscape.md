## 3. Positioning Against Competitors

### JMS Dev Lab positioning matrix

| Dimension | JMS Dev Lab | Typical Irish agency | Global freelancer | No-code platform |
|-----------|-------------|---------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Price | EUR 3K--12K fixed | EUR 15K--100K | EUR 1K--5K | EUR 0--500/mo |
| Communication | Direct with builder | Project manager layer | Timezone/language gaps | Self-service |
| Proof of delivery | 9 shipped production apps | Portfolio of client work | Portfolio varies | Templates |
| Shopify expertise | Deep (7 Shopify apps) | Usually theme-level | Varies | None |
| GDPR compliance | Built-in (EU-based) | Usually yes | Often unclear | Platform-dependent |
| Scalability | Full custom code | Full custom code | Varies | Limited |
| Ongoing support | EUR 300/mo retainer | EUR 1K+/mo | Ad hoc | Platform support |
| Timeline | 3--12 weeks | 8--24 weeks | 2--8 weeks | Days |

### Key competitive advantages

1. **Maker credibility:** 9 shipped, production-ready apps is unusual for a solo developer. Most freelancers have client work but few shipped products of their own.

2. **Fixed pricing:** EUR 3K / 6K / 12K tiers remove the "how much will this cost?" anxiety. Competitors typically quote per-project after lengthy discovery phases.

3. **Direct builder access:** No account managers, no project managers. The person quoting is the person coding. This matters for small businesses who want to talk to the actual developer.

4. **Niche expertise:** Three jewelry-specific apps demonstrate deep domain knowledge. No competitor has this combination of software skills and 22 years of jewelry retail experience.

5. **No VAT advantage:** Below the EUR 42,500 threshold means clients save 23% compared to VAT-registered competitors. Meaningful for price-sensitive SMEs.

6. **Scope-match guarantee:** "If the delivered product does not match the agreed scope, do not pay the final instalment." Reduces buyer risk.

## 4. Pricing Landscape

### Irish custom development market (2026)

| Project type | Typical Irish market range | JMS Dev Lab price |
|--------------|--------------------------|-------------------|
| Simple website / landing page | EUR 1,500--5,000 | Not offered (below target) |
| Spreadsheet replacement app | EUR 5,000--15,000 | EUR 3,000 (Starter tier) |
| Business portal / internal tool | EUR 10,000--30,000 | EUR 6,000 (Growth tier) |
| Custom SaaS application | EUR 25,000--80,000 | EUR 12,000 (Scale tier) |
| Enterprise system | EUR 50,000--500,000+ | Not targeted |
| Support retainer (monthly) | EUR 500--3,000/mo | EUR 300/mo |

### Why JMS Dev Lab is priced below market

- Solo operator with no overhead (no office, no employees, no agency costs)
- Pre-revenue business building portfolio and reputation
- LEO Grow Digital Voucher (50% up to EUR 5,000) makes the effective client cost even lower
- Goal is to win projects and build case studies, not maximise margin in year one

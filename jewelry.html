FAQ

Common Questions

Can I use all three apps together?

Yes. JewelryStudioManager, Jewel Value, and RepairDesk are all separate Shopify apps that work independently, but they're designed to complement each other. Install one or all three depending on which parts of your business need help.

Do I need to be on Shopify to use these apps?

Yes. All three apps are built as Shopify apps and integrate directly with your Shopify store, syncing customer data, order history, and products automatically.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Each app offers a free trial period so you can try it risk-free before committing to a subscription.

What if I only need one of the three apps?

Each app works perfectly on its own. Many jewelers start with the one that solves their biggest pain point and add others later as their needs grow.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're EU-based and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted, stored securely, and never shared with third parties. Your customer data stays yours.

Can I get a demo?

Yes. Get in touch and we'll walk you through any or all of the apps. No strings attached, no sales pressure.

LEO Vouchers for Digital Projects

Your Local Enterprise Office offers voucher schemes that can help fund websites, online trading tools, and business software. Here's what's available and how we might be able to help.

Discuss Your Project View the Schemes

Two LEO Voucher Schemes

The LEO runs two separate digital voucher programmes. Which one applies depends on your project.

Trading Online Voucher

For websites & online trading

Covers development or upgrade of an e-commerce website, online payments, booking systems, and online advertising.

  • • Up to €2,500 (50% of eligible costs)
  • • Can avail of up to 2 vouchers total
  • • Websites, e-commerce, online booking
  • • Online advertising (subject to limits)

Source: localenterprise.ie

Grow Digital Voucher

For business software & digital tools

Covers eligible business software subscriptions that are new to the business — stock control, payroll, AI tools, cyber security, and similar.

  • • Up to €5,000 (50% of eligible costs)
  • • Requires completing Digital for Business programme first
  • • Off-the-shelf software subscriptions (max 1 year)
  • • 1–50 employees, turnover under €10m

Source: localenterprise.ie

Important note

Eligibility for LEO vouchers depends on your business circumstances and the nature of your project. We recommend contacting your Local Enterprise Office directly to confirm what your project qualifies for before committing. We're happy to provide a quote that you can include with your application.

General Eligibility

Both schemes are generally available to:

  • ✓ Small businesses with 1 to 50 employees
  • ✓ Businesses based in Ireland
  • ✓ Turnover under €10 million (EU SME definition)

Additional requirements vary by scheme. The Grow Digital Voucher requires completing a Digital for Business programme. The Trading Online Voucher has its own criteria. Contact your LEO for full details.

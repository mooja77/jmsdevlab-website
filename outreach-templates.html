Outreach Email Templates

Internal reference document. Personalise every email — never copy-paste verbatim. Research the store before sending.

Rules: Always personalise the opening with something specific about their store (a product you liked, a design style, their location). Never send the same email twice. Keep it under 150 words. Send from hello@jmsdevlab.com.

Target: Custom jewelry studios on Shopify who take commissions

Subject: Quick question about how you track custom commissions

I came across [Store] and love your custom work — [specific compliment about a piece or their style].

I ask because we built a CRM specifically for custom jewelry studios on Shopify. It tracks commissions from enquiry to delivery, syncs with your Shopify customers, and gives clients a portal to follow their piece's progress.

Tracking: Log every email in the outreach spreadsheet with: Store name, Contact name, Email, Date sent, Template used, Opened (if trackable), Replied, Demo booked, Signed up.

Outreach Cadence

  • Week 1-2: 10 emails per day (50/week)
  • Day 5: Follow up with non-responders
  • Week 3+: 5-10 new prospects per week, continue follow-ups
  • Target: 200+ emails over 4 weeks

Where to Find Prospects

  • Google: site:myshopify.com jewelry, site:myshopify.com jeweler, site:myshopify.com watch repair
  • Shopify store directories (myip.ms)
  • Etsy top jewelry sellers who also have Shopify
  • Instagram hashtags: #customjewelry, #handmadejewelry, #jewelrydesigner, #benchjeweler
  • Facebook groups: member profiles often link to stores

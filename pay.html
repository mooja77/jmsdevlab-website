Pay Your Invoice

Secure payments powered by Stripe. Your payment link was sent to you by email — click it to pay in under a minute.

How It Works

Three simple steps to pay your invoice

1. Check Your Email

You'll receive an email from hello@jmsdevlab.com with your invoice details and a unique Stripe payment link.

2. Click & Pay

Click the payment link to open Stripe's secure checkout. Enter your card details or choose SEPA Direct Debit. That's it.

3. Get Your Receipt

Stripe sends an automatic receipt to your email. We're notified instantly and your project milestone moves forward.

Payment Schedule

Projects are split into three milestone payments

30% Deposit

Due before work begins. Confirms the project and locks in your timeline. Paid after scope agreement and contract signing.

40% Mid-Project

Due at the mid-project review. You'll see working progress, provide feedback, and confirm direction before the second payment.

30% On Completion

Due after final delivery and your sign-off. Covered by our scope-match guarantee — if it doesn't match the agreed scope, you don't pay.

Safe & Secure

Your payments are protected at every step

PCI Level 1 Certified

Stripe holds the highest level of payment security certification. Your card details are encrypted and never stored on our servers.

3D Secure & SCA

All card payments use Strong Customer Authentication as required by EU regulations. Your bank verifies every transaction.

GDPR Compliant

Stripe processes payments in full compliance with EU data protection regulations. Your personal data is handled securely.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my payment link?

Your payment link was sent to you by email from hello@jmsdevlab.com. Each link is unique to your project and milestone amount. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for the most recent invoice email. If you can't find it, get in touch and we'll resend it.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and SEPA Direct Debit for EU bank transfers. SEPA is often cheaper for larger amounts.

Is it safe to pay through the link?

Yes. The payment link takes you to a Stripe-hosted checkout page. Stripe is a PCI Level 1 certified payment processor — the same platform used by companies like Shopify, Amazon, and Google. Your card details never touch our website.

Will I get a receipt?

Yes. Stripe automatically sends a receipt to the email address you provide during checkout. You can also access your receipt from the Stripe confirmation page immediately after payment.

Can I pay by bank transfer instead?

Yes. Choose SEPA Direct Debit on the Stripe checkout page for direct bank payment within the EU. If you need to pay by manual bank transfer, contact us at hello@jmsdevlab.com and we'll provide our bank details.

What currency are payments in?

All invoices and payments are in Euro (EUR). If your card is in a different currency, your bank will handle the conversion at their standard rate.

What is the scope-match guarantee?

If the delivered software doesn't match the agreed scope, you don't pay the final instalment. We agree the scope in writing before work starts, so there are no surprises. See our pricing page for full details.

Can't Find Your Payment Link?

No problem. Get in touch and we'll resend your invoice with a fresh payment link.

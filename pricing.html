Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

What if my project doesn't fit these packages?

These are starting points, not rigid boxes. Most projects are scoped individually based on your specific needs. The packages give you a sense of what's typical — your quote will be based on what you actually need.

How do payments work?

Typically: 30% upfront, 40% at mid-point (when you see working software), and 30% on delivery. This protects both sides — you never pay for work you haven't seen.

What if I need changes after launch?

Every project includes a post-launch support period (30–90 days depending on the package). After that, you can continue with a monthly support retainer or request changes on a project basis.

Is custom software really cheaper than Airtable or Bubble?

Over time, often yes. A team of 20 on Airtable's Team plan costs €4,800/year and growing with every new hire. Custom software hosting stays at ~€360/year regardless of team size. At 10+ users, custom typically breaks even within 3–5 years. Plus you own it, there's no platform lock-in, and your data stays in the EU.

Can I apply for the LEO Grow Digital Voucher?

If your business has 1–50 employees and has been trading for at least 6 months, you may be eligible. The voucher can cover 50% of eligible digital project costs, up to €5,000. You'll need to complete a Digital for Business programme before applying. We're happy to help you understand if your project qualifies.

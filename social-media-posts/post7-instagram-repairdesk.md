## Caption

"Is my watch ready yet?"

If you run a jewellery or watch repair workshop, you hear this 10 times a day. Customers call because they have no idea what stage their repair is at.

Paper tickets don't solve this. A spreadsheet doesn't solve this.

What solves it: a digital pipeline where the customer gets an automatic notification every time their piece moves to a new stage.

Received > Diagnosed > Quoted > Approved > In Progress > Ready for Collection > Collected

When the customer already knows the status, they stop calling. Your phone goes quiet. Your Google reviews go up (because people value being kept in the loop).

We built RepairDesk for exactly this. Runs on Shopify. Built by a former jeweller who lived this problem for 22 years.

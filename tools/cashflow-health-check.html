    What Merchants Are Doing About It

    Shopify merchants who take control of their cashflow tend to share a few common habits: they separate their operating cash from profit early, they track payout timing alongside order volume, and they build a buffer of at least 2–3 months of fixed costs.

    Some use spreadsheets, some use dedicated tools, and some work with their accountant to set up simple dashboards. The important thing is having visibility — because cashflow problems rarely appear overnight. They build up quietly until a slow month, a delayed payout, or an unexpected expense turns a healthy-looking business into a scramble.
